 What this says strategically
 VERDICT and the Boundaries are structurally small but strategically heavy. They matter out of proportion to their size because they govern goal validation, uncertainty, reversibility, legal/ethical checks, and hard-stop architecture constraints. CIPHER is smaller and more specialized: it is highly valuable where email risk exists, but it does not dominate general workflow because its scope is intentionally narrow. APEX is not underweightedit is the framework's largest investment. FORGE sits in the middle: strong, valuable, and worth expanding where portability or broader general-use packaging would help.
